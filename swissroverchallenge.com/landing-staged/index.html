What is the Swiss Rover Challenge?

The Swiss Rover Challenge is a national competition for students to design, build and operate Mars rover prototypes.



Our goal is to empower the next generation of engineers, scientists, and explorers through hands-on challenges designed to test their rovers, their team spirit and their engineering.
