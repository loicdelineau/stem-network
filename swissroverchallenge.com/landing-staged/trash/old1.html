What is the Swiss Rover Challenge?

The Swiss Rover Challenge is a national competition for university students to design, build and operate Mars rover prototypes. Our goal is to empower the next generation of engineers, scientists, and explorers through hands-on challenges simulating real extraterrestrial tasks.
